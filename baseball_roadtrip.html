"The one constant through all the years, Ray, has been baseball. America has rolled by like an army of steamrollers. It's been erased like a blackboard, rebuilt, and erased again. But baseball has marked the time. This field, this game, is a part of our past, Ray. It reminds us of all that once was good, and that could be again. Oh people will come, Ray. People will most definitely come."  -- Field of Dreams

Baseball Roadtrip is an idea I've had for ten years.  Two college friends drove across the country stopping to see a handful of major league games in several cities in 1992.  Since then I've tinkered with the idea of seeing a game in every major league ballpark around the country (and Canada).  Time, money, circumstances, personal and professional obligations have kept me from starting out on this little adventure in the past but now it's begun.  Come take a peek at where I'm at...
